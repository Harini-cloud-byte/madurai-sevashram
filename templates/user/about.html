Children's Home at Madurai Sevashram

A prestigious welfare organization, Madurai Sevashram Children's Home is committed to protecting, caring for, and empowering impoverished mothers and children. The organization was started by T.V.S. Soundarama and Pankajam Rajam with the intention of uplifting underprivileged girls and giving them a safe and supportive environment that fosters development, learning, and personal growth.

For girls from low-income families, the home provides a secure place where they receive care, guidance, and opportunities to live meaningful and successful lives.

About the Home

Madurai Sevashram is a specialized girls' home that provides emotional care, education, and shelter. Currently around 31 girls stay in the home, which has the capacity to accommodate 36 girls.

Each resident receives personal attention to meet her emotional, educational, and physical needs. The safe and structured environment ensures that every girl feels respected, protected, and empowered.

The organization focuses on overall development including academic learning, communication skills, interpersonal relationships, time management, and independence.

Supervision & Management

Madurai Sevashram operates under the supervision of the Home Management Committee (HMC) and in coordination with the District Child Protection Unit (DCPU).

These governing bodies ensure the safety, welfare, and development of all girls living in the home. Regular inspections, planning, and evaluations help maintain high standards of care, education, and security.

Activities & Programs

  • Education & Academic Support – Girls receive schooling, tutoring, and academic guidance.
  • Life Skill Development – Training in decision-making, hygiene, financial literacy, and computer basics.
  • Cultural Activities – Sports, arts, music, and dance programs.
  • Counseling & Emotional Support – Guidance from mentors and counselors.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a society where underprivileged girls have access to education, protection, and opportunities for personal growth.

Mission

  • Provide safe shelter and care for vulnerable girls.
  • Ensure access to education and academic support.
  • Develop confidence and life skills.
  • Improve quality of life through welfare programs.
  • Encourage community participation.

Impact & Achievements

Madurai Sevashram has transformed many young lives. Several girls who stayed at the home have completed their education and moved forward into successful careers.

By focusing on education, character development, and life skills, the home empowers girls to break the cycle of poverty and build confident futures.
